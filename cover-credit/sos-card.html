FAQ

Questions people actually ask

Is this tool really free? What's the catch? ▼
Completely free. No login, no email, no payment ever. Cover Credit earns when we help you buy or review insurance — not from this tool.
Does my data get stored anywhere? ▼
No. Everything happens inside your browser. Nothing you type is sent to our servers. When you close the tab, the data is gone.
Can I use it for any insurer — not just the ones listed? ▼
Yes. Select "Other" in the insurer dropdown and manually type your helpline. Works for every insurer in India.
Why can't WhatsApp receive the image directly without downloading? ▼
WhatsApp's web API only accepts pre-filled text — no image uploads via browser links. So we download the card as a PNG, then open WhatsApp. Just attach the image and send. Same result in 5 seconds.
What should I do after generating the card? ▼
For vehicle: download PDF → print → laminate → keep in dashboard. For health: send image via WhatsApp to family. For life: print and keep with documents, share with nominee. Regenerate whenever you renew your policy.
Not sure if I have the right covers. How do I check? ▼
That's exactly what Cover Credit does for free. Our advisors read your actual policy document, identify missing covers like IMT-23, Zero Dep, GIT, and advise you at renewal. No pressure, no fees — just honest advice.
