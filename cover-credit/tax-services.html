Key Deadlines

Never miss a deadline again.

We start 3–4 weeks early on every filing — so you're never rushed or penalised at the last minute.

ITR Filing (Individuals)
Due: 31 July every year
Critical
Late filing penalty ₹5,000 u/s 234F. Don't wait.
GSTR-3B (Monthly)
Due: 20th of every month
Monthly
Interest at 18% p.a. on late payment of tax liability.
GSTR-1 (Monthly)
Due: 11th of every month
Monthly
Late fee ₹50/day (₹20/day for nil returns).
Tax Audit (u/s 44AB)
Due: 30 September every year
Critical
Penalty 0.5% of turnover or ₹1.5L, whichever is lower.
ROC Annual Filing (AOC-4)
Due: 30 October every year
Annual
₹100 additional fee per day after the due date.
ITR — Business / Audit Cases
Due: 31 October every year
Extended
Applicable where tax audit is mandatory under the Act.
