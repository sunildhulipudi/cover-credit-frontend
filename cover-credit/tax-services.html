Frequently Asked

Questions we always hear.

How do I share my documents with you? Is it safe? ▼
You can share documents via WhatsApp or email — whatever is easiest for you. We handle all client data with strict confidentiality and never share it with any third party. Your financial information is completely safe with us.
What documents do I need for ITR filing? ▼
For most salaried individuals, you'll need your Form 16 (from employer), PAN card, Aadhaar card, bank account details, and any investment proof (80C, health insurance etc.). For business owners, we'll also need your profit & loss details. We'll guide you through exactly what's needed for your specific case.
What is the last date for filing ITR this year? ▼
For individuals and salaried employees, the deadline is 31st July every year. For businesses, it's 31st October. We start working on your return 3–4 weeks before the deadline to ensure everything is filed on time without any penalty.
My business turnover is low — do I still need GST registration? ▼
GST registration is mandatory if your turnover exceeds ₹40 lakhs (for goods) or ₹20 lakhs (for services) in a year. However, even if you're below the threshold, voluntary registration can benefit you — it allows you to claim input tax credit and makes your business look more professional to clients. We'll assess your specific situation in a free consultation.
How much do your services cost? ▼
We believe in transparent, upfront pricing — no hidden fees or surprises. ITR filing starts from ₹499, GST return filing from ₹799/month, and GST registration from ₹1,499. Company registration starts from ₹5,999. We'll give you an exact quote before you commit to anything. Book a free consultation to discuss your needs.
I received a tax notice. Can you help? ▼
Yes, absolutely. Tax notices can be stressful but are usually resolvable with the right response. We review the notice, identify the issue, prepare the correct response, and guide you through the resolution process. Most notices are resolved without penalty when handled properly and on time.
