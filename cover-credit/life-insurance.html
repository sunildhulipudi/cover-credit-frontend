Questions we answer
every single day

How much life insurance cover do I actually need?+

Standard rule: 10 to 15 times your annual income. But we calculate precisely — based on outstanding loans, dependents, education costs, spouse income, lifestyle. Most families in AP need between ₹75L and ₹2 Crore. Book a free session and we calculate your exact number in 10 minutes.

LIC better hai ya private insurer?+

LIC has strong government backing and public trust. But private insurers like HDFC Life, ICICI Pru, Max Life often have higher claim ratios (98–99%), faster processing (7–15 days vs 30+), and significantly lower premiums for the same cover. We compare both honestly.

Diabetes ya BP hai — kya life cover milega?+

Yes, absolutely. The insurer will either accept with a premium loading of 10 to 30% extra, or a temporary exclusion, or a specialist plan. Full honest disclosure is required. We find the insurer most favourable for your specific health profile.

Should I add riders to my term plan?+

Three riders are worth adding: (1) Accidental Death Benefit — doubles payout if death is accidental, just ₹50–100/month extra. (2) Critical Illness — cancer, heart attack etc par lump sum. Highly recommended. (3) Waiver of Premium — future premiums are waived if you become disabled. We explain every rider clearly.

What documents do I need to buy a term plan?+

Aadhaar (identity + address), PAN (tax), last 3 months salary slips or ITR for income proof, and a passport photograph. Above ₹50L cover or age above 40, a medical test may be required. We guide you through every single requirement.

Kya policy cancel karke refund mil sakta hai?+

15-day free look period — cancel within 15 days for a full refund. After that, term plans have no surrender value. ULIPs and endowment plans have surrender values after 3 to 5 years. We explain every detail clearly before you buy.
