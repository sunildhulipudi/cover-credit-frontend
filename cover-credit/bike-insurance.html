What's Covered in Bike Insurance

  • Accidental damage to your bike
  • Theft or total loss
  • Third-party property damage & bodily injury
  • Natural disasters (floods, earthquake, fire)
  • Personal accident cover for rider
  • Add-on: Zero Depreciation, Engine Protection, Roadside Assistance
