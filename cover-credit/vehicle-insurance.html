Third-party is the legal minimum â€” it covers damage to others. But it does not cover your own vehicle at all. What is Zero Depreciation and should I add it?+

Standard comprehensive insurance deducts depreciation from your claim payout â€” meaning you pay part of the repair out of pocket. How does NCB (No Claim Bonus) work?+

Every year you do not make a claim, you earn a discount on your premium â€” starting at 20% and going up to 50% after 5 consecutive claim-free years. This NCB belongs to you, not the vehicle.
